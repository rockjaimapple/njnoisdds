Statement of the Problem

Your Answer:
How does the height at which the string is lifted affect the time that the wave that forms touches the wall?
Expected Answer:

  Awarded points: 2.00

Hypothesis

Your Answer:

If you increase the height of the string (2, 4, 6 cm), then the time taken for the wave (in seconds) to touch the wall will decrease. This is because when the string is lifted higher and higher, gravity will act on the wave with more force. When height increases, the gravitational potential energy increases and it will travel down the string faster.
